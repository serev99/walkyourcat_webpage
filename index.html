Concept

Welcome to the world "Walk Your Cat"! In this technologically advanced era, you'll embark on an exciting journey with your adorable virtual feline companion. But beware, even though your furry friend may be fictional, the fun is very real!

Why not try out the latest in cat play technology? Grab your laser pointer and guide your virtual cat through a myriad of delightful challenges, both in the real world and the digital realm. Are you ready to unleash your imagination and embark on an infinite fantasy journey? Let the games begin!

Demo

Walkthrough

Players commence their experience in augmented reality (AR) by employing a laser pointer to interact with their virtual cat. Through simple pointing gestures, players engage in an entertaining chase game, as the virtual cat follows the laser's trajectory endlessly.

As gameplay progresses, players encounter a captivating portal that transports them and their virtual companion into the virtual reality (VR) realm. Fascinated by their cat's curiosity, players and their virtual pet embark on an exploration of a visually stunning world characterized by floating islands, enveloped in mist, and illuminated by the presence of auroras.

Upon arrival, the cat displays signs of apprehension in this unfamiliar environment, pacing nervously in circles on an isolated island. To facilitate their return to reality, players must utilize their laser pointer to strategically create portals along the cat's path, guiding it back to the real world. This action also ensures their own safe return, allowing for continued exploration together.

With each return to reality, players have the opportunity to once again utilize their laser pointer, initiating an ongoing journey alongside their virtual cat companion.
